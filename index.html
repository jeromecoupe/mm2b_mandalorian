Home planet

Mandalore

Mandalore is a planet located in the Outer Rim Territories. It is the homeworld of the Mandalorians and of the indigenous Alamite species.

The planetary surface was once covered with jungles, forests, seas, mountains and lakes, but was eventually transformed into a lifeless desert by centuries of war.

In the series, Moff Gideon establishes a secret base of operations on Mandalore before being discovered and finally defeated.

Famous hero

Boba Fett

Boba Fett is an unaltered clone of Jango Fett, a famous Mandalorian bounty hunter. Like his father, Boba became a bounty hunter and crime lord.

Trained in combat and martial skills from a young age, Fett was one of the most feared bounty hunters in the galaxy during the reign of Emperor Palpatine. During his long career, he worked for both the Empire and the criminal underworld.

Concieved first as a secondary character in the original trilogy, Boba Fett became popular with Star Wars fans to the point it now has a dedicated miniseries.
